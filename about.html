About Us

The people living at Eskadern is a diverse group of people.

Some of us have a lot of experience planting everything from flowers to food, and some came with no experience but with curiosity and a little dream.

We are all just helping each other and learning together.

On top of being a place where people garden together, this is also a meeting place, a place where we can create connections.

We always have room for new people and no matter your individual situation you are welcome.

No matter your ability level or variation, gardening is something everyone can do.

So feel free to contact us throught here or check us out on Facebook, Instagram or Twitter.
